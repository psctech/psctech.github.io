    Toolbar Buttons

    Map Display Themes

    Map Display Themes quickly toggle different mapping styles. The map you want to see when driving might be different than the map you want to see in an incident command mode. The current map themes are:

    • Offline Maps
      Offline maps are available regardless of your network connection. The offline maps are downloaded to your device, and will generally display faster than online maps.
      • Offline Map. This is the default map display theme. It shows all basic information in the City, and the hydrants, FDCs, and Knox boxes will turn on by default when zoomed in close enough.
      • Offline Preplan. This map is generally the same as the Offline Map, but adds some additional preplan information from FirstDueSizeUp when zoomed in.
      • Offline Aerial. This map is the same as the Offline Map when zoomed out, but will automatically display aerial photos when zoomed in a certain amount. It will be much faster than the Online Aerial map, but will potentially be more out of date, and will not show outside of the City boundary.
    • Online Maps
      Online maps are available when you have an internet connection. The online maps can be more up to date than offline maps, but may take longer to load. Online maps will show areas well beyond the borders of the City if you every need to respond outside of the National Capital Region.
      • Online Map. Basic street and building information provided by an outside vendor (ESRI). Will display for areas outside of the NCR, but will take longer to load, or will not work depending on your internet connectivity. NOTE: Our hydrants, FDCs, and Knox boxes will display when zoomed in.
      • Online Aerial. Aerial photography information provided by an outside vendor (ESRI). Will display for areas outside of the NCR, but will take longer to load, or will not work depending on your internet connectivity. NOTE: Our hydrants, FDCs, and Knox boxes will display when zoomed in.

    Zoom with My Unit

    You can use the Zoom with My Unit button () to instruct Mobile to show both your unit and the location. When used from the toolbar, it will adjust the zoom to always show your unit along with the location designated in the lower right of the screen. The button is red () when this tracking mode is active. Tap it again to toggle it off.

    Vehicle Tracking

    Being in the wrong vehicle tracking mode can lead to a lot of frustration. When attempting to view the incident location on the map, tracking modes might keep moving the map back to your vehicle, which may not be desired.

    By default, vehicle tracking is turned off, i.e. in Normal tracking mode (). You can pan around the map, and your current location will not affect the map zoom/center.

    Clicking once will toggle on Center My Unit tracking mode (). The map will follow your vehicle as it moves, but it will not zoom or rotate.

    Clicking again will toggle on Navigation Mode (). The map will follow your vehicle as it moves, and it will rotate to always show the direction you are facing. This is the default mode when you initiate driving directions.

    Clicking again will return the tracking to Normal mode ().

    Zoom Buttons

    The map can be zoomed in and out using the +/- buttons. The map will zoom in/out by a set amount each time you click the button. You can also use the pinch gesture to zoom in/out.

    Zoom Levels

    The Zoom Levels button () allows you to jump to predefined levels without changing the center of your map. They are general guidelines.

    Given that multi-touch "pinch-to-zoom" is available in this map, the use of zoom levels may be less advantageous.

    Spatial Bookmarks

    The Spatial Bookmarks button () allows you to jump to specified views (zoom and center) of the map. Right now, there's only a bookmark for a general city overview, which may be handy if you ever find you need to "reset" the view of the map.

    If you have ideas for other spatial bookmarks that you believe will benefit everyone, reach out to Fire IT.

    Info Button

    The Info button () will show you map information for the point that you click on the map. When activated, the button will highlight in red (). Any map touches when it's active will open the information panel.

    The information panel can show details about map layers, unit rosters, and call notes.

    Assigned Units Display

    The map can be configured to show which units are associated with the incident. When zoomed out, this may contribute to "visual clutter," and can be toggled off by using the Roll-Up/-Down button ().

    Map Orientation

    While the map will normally be in "North-Up" orientation, certain map tracking modes may result in the map being oriented in another direction. The "North-Up" button will revert the orientation to the default.

    Note: The map can be "twisted" manually if you use two fingers to rotate it.

    Map Themes

    Toggle Aerial Photos

    Aerial photography can be toggled on by using a Map Display Theme. When this map theme is enabled, the aerial photos are configured to turn on/off based on your zoom level.

    Toggle Preplan Map

    The default Offline Map only shows hydrants, FDCs, and Knox boxes. Switching to the Offline Preplan map will turn on additional preplan features when zoomed in. Right now, these include stairwells, elevators, FACPs, and shutoffs. Contact Fire IT if there is interest in expanding this set of icons.

    Working with Addresses

    Search Address

    The "Search the Map" box can be used to quickly find an address. Right now, the results will only show street addresses, but in the future we plan to add common place names for easy search and reference. Selecting a result will center the map over the location - a details pane will fly-out from the left and give options to get driving directions (), track your unit in relation to the address (), or recenter on the address ().

    Address Directions

    When viewing an address, you can select the navigate button () to get driving directions. There are a few things to note:

    1. The map tracking mode will change to Navigation Mode (). Clicking this again will switch the map back to Normal Mode()
    2. You can hide and expand the navigation pane without ending the navigation.
    3. You can click on the steps in the navigation pane to jump to that instruction on the map.
    4. The route is displayed on the map in a dashed blue line.

    Zoom with Unit

    When viewing an address, you can select the Zoom with Unit button () to show your unit in relation to the address. When in this mode, the map will keep auto-zooming to a level that shows your unit and the target address at the same time -- when you move closer, the map will zoom closer. Click the Zoom with Unit button () in the right-side toolbar to exit this mode of tracking.

    Recenter Address

    When viewing an address, you can select the Recenter button () to zoom and center the map on the selected address.
